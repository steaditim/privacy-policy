Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06.04.2026 This Privacy Policy describes how CameraShot handles your information when you use the CameraShot mobile application (the "App"). 1. Introduction We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our guiding principle is that your data belongs to you. We do not operate our own servers to store your personal content, and we do not track your activity across other apps or websites. 2. Data Storage and iCloud Sync To provide a seamless experience across your iOS and macOS devices, the App utilizes Apple’s **iCloud** services (CloudKit). Your Content:** All data you create within the App is stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud account. Encryption:** Data stored in iCloud is encrypted by Apple. We cannot access, read, or modify your data stored in your private iCloud container. Control:** You can enable or disable iCloud sync for this App at any time through your device's System Settings. 3. Information We Collect Personal Data:** We do **not** collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Usage Data:** We do not collect any usage data. 4. Third-Party Services The App relies on the following third-party services: Apple iCloud: For data synchronization and storage. Please refer to [Apple’s Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details on how they handle data. 5. Data Retention and Deletion Since we do not store your data on our own servers, we cannot delete it for you. You have full control: Deleting data within the App will remove it from both the local device and your iCloud sync. Deleting the App may remove local data, but data in iCloud will remain until manually deleted via your iCloud storage settings. 6. Children’s Privacy The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since data is stored in the user's own iCloud account, use by children is subject to the parental controls and Apple ID policies managed by the legal guardian. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. 8.
